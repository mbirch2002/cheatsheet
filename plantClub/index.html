About Us

The London Plant Club is for plant lovers looking for a place to share their passion for plants. We schedule monthly events around the city centered around plants, ranging from garden tours to workshops. On top of the events, our forum is a great place to share tips and tricks, such as where to buy the best plants, or how to keep your plants healthy and thriving. If you are a beginner plant-parent, this is also a great place to find advice from more experienced members.
